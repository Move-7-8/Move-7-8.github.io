City Latitude vs. Humidity

This visualisation examines the relationship between the latitude of randomly selected cities throughout the world and compares their latitude with the Humidity recorded in a given year. The visualisation is designed to examine the nature of the relaionship between these two variables.
There is no statistically significant correlation between latitude and Humidity for both the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.
